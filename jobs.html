Position Title:

Web Developer

Brief Description of the Position:

We are seeking a skilled Web Developer to design, implement, and maintain web applications. The successful candidate will collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality digital solutions that enhance user experience and meet business objectives.

Salary Range:

$65,000 - $85,000 per year

Reports To:

Senior Web Development Manager

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Develop, test, and deploy web applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and relevant frameworks.
  2. Collaborate with designers and product managers to translate UI/UX designs into functional web applications.
  3. Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.
  4. Maintain and improve existing web applications and troubleshoot issues.
  5. Conduct code reviews and ensure adherence to best practices.
  6. Stay updated with emerging technologies and industry trends.

Required Qualifications:

Essential:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • 3+ years of experience in web development.
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and at least one modern framework (e.g., React, Angular, Vue.js).
  • Experience with server-side languages (e.g., Node.js, PHP, Python).
  • Strong understanding of responsive design and cross-browser compatibility.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Preferable:

  • Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git).
  • Experience with content management systems (e.g., WordPress, Drupal).
  • Knowledge of web performance optimization techniques.
  • Understanding of SEO principles.
  • Previous experience in an Agile development environment.

Position Title:

Network Engineer

Brief Description of the Position:

We are looking for a skilled Network Engineer to design, implement, and maintain our network infrastructure. The successful candidate will ensure the reliability and security of our network systems while collaborating with IT teams to support organisational goals.

Salary Range:

$75,000 - $95,000 per year

Reports To:

Senior Networking Manager

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Design and deploy network solutions that meet organisational needs.
  2. Monitor network performance and troubleshoot issues to ensure reliability.
  3. Implement security measures to protect network data and infrastructure.
  4. Collaborate with IT teams to integrate network services with other systems.
  5. Maintain documentation of network configurations and changes.
  6. Stay updated with emerging networking technologies and best practices.

Required Qualifications:

Essential:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field.
  • 3+ years of experience in network engineering or administration.
  • Proficiency in networking protocols (e.g., TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP).
  • Experience with network hardware (e.g., routers, switches, firewalls).
  • Strong understanding of network security principles and practices.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Preferable:

  • Certifications such as CCNA, CCNP, or CompTIA Network+.
  • Experience with cloud networking solutions (e.g., AWS, Azure).
  • Familiarity with network monitoring tools and software.
  • Knowledge of VoIP and video conferencing technologies.
  • Previous experience in an Agile development environment.

Cybersecurity Analyst Job Description

Position Description Reference Number:

-------------------

Position Title:

Cybersecurity Analyst

Brief Description of the Position:

The Cybersecurity Analyst will be responsible for safeguarding the organisation’s information systems and data. This role involves monitoring, detecting, and responding to security incidents, conducting vulnerability assessments, and implementing security measures to protect against cyber threats. The successful candidate will play a critical role in maintaining the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of the company's information assets.

Salary Range:

$75,000 - $95,000 per year

Reports To:

Cybersecurity Manager

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Monitor network traffic for suspicious activity and respond to security incidents.
  2. Conduct regular vulnerability assessments and penetration testing to identify weaknesses.
  3. Implement and manage security tools and technologies (e.g., firewalls, intrusion detection systems).
  4. Develop and maintain security policies, procedures, and documentation.
  5. Collaborate with IT teams to ensure secure system configurations and compliance with security standards.
  6. Provide training and awareness programs for employees on cybersecurity best practices.
  7. Analyze security breaches to determine root causes and recommend improvements.
  8. Stay current with emerging threats and security trends to proactively mitigate risks.

Required Qualifications:

Essential:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Minimum of 3 years of experience in cybersecurity or information security roles.
  • Strong knowledge of security protocols, cryptography, and risk management.
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++.
  • Experience with security tools and technologies (e.g., SIEM, firewalls, antivirus software).
  • Familiarity with regulatory requirements (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS).
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.

Preferable:

  • Relevant certifications such as Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH), or CompTIA Security+.
  • Experience with cloud security and securing cloud environments (e.g., AWS, Azure).
  • Knowledge of incident response and digital forensics.
  • Familiarity with network protocols and architecture.
  • Previous experience in a security operations centre (SOC) environment.
